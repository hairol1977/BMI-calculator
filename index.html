BMI (Body Mass Index)

It's a measurement used to assess whether a person
has a healthy weight in relation to their height.

Categorizes:

  • ✘ Underweight: BMI below 18.5
  • ✔ Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • ✘ Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • ✘ Obese: BMI of 30 or above
